Funding your Education

There are many ways to fund your education. For traditional education pathways, you can employ federal student loans, private student loans, and a variety of different scholarships.

Loans

Federal Student Loans

Federal student loans are the easiest loans to acquire. By filling out your FAFSA, you will learn if you qualify for Federal grants and various federal student loans. There are four types of federal student loans: Direct Subsidized Loans, Direct Unsubsidized loans, Direct PLUS Loans, and Direct Consolidation Loans.
